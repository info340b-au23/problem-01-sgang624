Silvio Dante

A cunning and level-headed capo, Silvio Dante is the right-hand man to Tony Soprano in the DiMeo crime family on HBO's The Sopranos. He is portrayed by Steven Van Zandt, who is actually a member of Bruce Springsteen's E Street Band in real life.

    Admirable Traits
  • Very level-headed in tough situations
  • Street smart and knows how to earn lots of money
  • Loyal and does not let his peers down
